Brief About Us

Is a foundation established with the aim to promote the quality of education in country and produce well sufficient graduated in different angles, The main founder of this Foundation is Nyambari Nyangwine who stands as the chairman of the foundation but also the chairman of Nyambari Nyangwine Group of Companies, historically Nyambari Nyangwine holds the legancy in the education sector by publishing more than 300 text books that have been so useful in the education system and used by students in daily learning, as a retied Member of parliament of Musoma, Nyambari Nyangwine, during his time of service in the parliament used his knowledge to explore and rise voice on implementation of better education policies and infrastructure, as by now, he has decided to keep his efforts of contributing to his nation by building the education sector through his foundation which is the Nyambari Nyangwine Foundation

AREAS OF FOCUS

MATERIALS AVAILABILITY (TEXT BOOKS SUPPLY)

Our aim is to make sure teaching materials for teachers are available but also learning materials for students are available as well, we are at least aiming to make sure more than 100,000 students will have free text book of different subjects from our foundation per year and this will be done through our efforts and efforts of our partners.

TRAINING TO TEACHERS (CONTINOUS TRAINING)

We understand that skills are always dynamic and not statis, as teachers in majority, we know that teaching skills should match the current time and not be forced by the time knowledge was acquired, giving out training to as many teachers as we can we help to impact relevant quality education to our students, therefore we carry out a duty of making sure we give trainings or organize different trainings to various institutions such as primary teachers, secondary teachers, advanced level teachers, university lecturers, educational institutional workers and so on, focusing on providing more well skilled and qualitfull students and teachers.

EDUCATION POLICIES AND INFRASTRUCTURE

Since we are working for the public, we respect that the public is governed by authorities and these authorities are responsible for enacting laws or policies but also governing the implementation of these laws and policies, in the society of good policies, they might be a better environment for practising better education, sufficient and practical but in the society where policies are not favourable, there is always a long way to go on creating strong and impactful education that will exactly impact life of graduates, as an institute that has involved itself in education form more tan 18 years we stand with all others who believe in well enacted laws and policies and therefore we shall also use our knowledge and research to write publications on education laws and policies, conduct seminars, debates, discussions, to advice education authorities, law enforces and other internal and international education authorities for the betterment of improving education across the country and the world as well, we believe through the use of Information technology our goal we achieve in a more better way.

FUNDING FOR EDUCATION DEVELOPMENT

With recognising the efforts done by the government and her partners to providing the budget of education the ministry concerned, we still recognise the fact that so much is still needed to fund different angles of education, we are purposely aiming to raise fund through our resource and our partners in order to fund more other education angles or areas, our first priority is on educational start-ups using information communication technology to improve education, but also funding different educational projects, funding different students on school fees and accommodations, scholarships in general to students, teachers and more other categories.

PROJECTS & PROGRAMS (ACTIVITIES)

• Supply of materials or text books We have suppled more than 50,000 free textbooks since January 2022, making more than 100,000 students to have access to reading a textbook by two students using one book, our vision being to give out more than 100,000 text books per year free to students • Trainings and seminars to teachers We have conducted various training across the countries to primary and secondary teachers, exposing them to the use of ICT in teaching and we have done that in regions like Musoma, Mwanza, Dar es salaam and Dodoma, we are aiming to conduct many more seminars, trainings, educational debates on the coming year • Funds We are focusing to collect funds to fund different education start ups but also various education projects, through our main company which is Nyambari Nyangwine Group of Companies, we aim to contribute some funds but also rise funds from other partners and support those groups.
